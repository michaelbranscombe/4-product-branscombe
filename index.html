Parts

Building a computer is a vigorous and sometimes dangerous process. That's why we're here to help you make sure your build runs like a dream. First, you'll need parts. The parts of a computer you will need are:

  • Processor
  • Hard drive
  • Power supply
  • Case(like the NZXT H700i)
  • Motherboard(our new Z7-Z37XT board is the most powerful on the market)
  • RAM, or memory
  • A video card, or GPU
  • A DVD drive, though this is not a requirement

These are the components that will take your game from 60fps 720p to 144fps 1440p. Using our parts and our recommended other components your computer will be in tip top shape for years to come.
