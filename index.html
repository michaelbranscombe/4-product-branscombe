Build your Own PC

Parts

Building a computer is a vigorous and sometimes dangerous process. That's why we're here to help you make sure your build runs like a dream. First, you'll need parts. The parts of a computer you will need are:

  • Processor, or CPU
  • Hard drive
  • Power supply
  • Case(like the NZXT H700i)
  • Motherboard(our new Z7-Z37XT board is the most powerful on the market)
  • RAM, or memory
  • A video card, or GPU
  • A DVD drive, though this is not a requirement
  • A CPU cooler(the NZXT Kraken X62 will keep temps down and frames up)

These are the components that will take your game from 60fps 720p to 144fps 1440p. Using our parts and our recommended other components your computer will be in tip top shape for years to come.

Temperature Control

With games getting more and more CPU intensive, it can put stress on your motherboard as well. Using the NZXT Kraken X62 and the NZXT AER RGB fans, your CPU will stay cool and your skills will stay hot. The Kraken is designed as an All-In-One CPU liquid cooler that controls the temperature of your CPU to your liking. If you use the NZXT Hue+, you'll also be able to control the lighting on both the cooler and the fans, so not only do you have control over your temps, you also have the style points to make your setup Reddit-worthy!
